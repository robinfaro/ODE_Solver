Detailed Description

A class for solving ordinary differential equations (ODEs) using the Runge-Kutta method.

The Runge-Kutta methods are numerical solver of ordinary differential equations (ODEs). The generic formulation of an explicit Runge-Kutta method of order \( s \) is given by:

\[ y_{n+1} = y_n + h \sum_{i=1}^{s} b_i k_i \]

where the stages \( k_i \) are defined as:

\[ k_i = f\left(t_n + c_i h, y_n + h \sum_{j=1}^{i-1} a_{ij} k_j\right), \quad i = 1, \dots, s \]

The coefficients \( a_{ij} \), \( b_i \), and \( c_i \) are represented by:

\[ \begin{array}{c|cccc} c_1 & 0 & 0 & 0 & \cdots \\ c_2 & a_{21} & 0 & 0 & \cdots \\ c_3 & a_{31} & a_{32} & 0 & \cdots \\ \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \ddots \\ c_s & a_{s1} & a_{s2} & a_{s3} & \cdots \\ \hline & b_1 & b_2 & b_3 & \cdots & b_s \end{array} \]
